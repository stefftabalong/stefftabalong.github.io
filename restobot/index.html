Restobot (Major Design Overhaul)

Description

Restobot is a Restaurant Management System in the Philippines used by well known restaurants like 71 Gramercy Cafe, Publiko, Buffalos Wings & Things, Pho Hoa, etc.

I was incharge of all aspects of the front-end & design of the system. From rebranding, landing, mailer to dashboard.

My goal was to create a clean minimalist design at the same time improve the website's user experience
