Medgate iOS (UI/UX)

Description

This is a proposed iOS design for a client that wants a redesign of their application. He asked for a modern, lively colors, and a simplistic feel.

This application is catered to anyone who has a health card, my goal was to make the user interface as user-friendly and direct to the point as possible without compromising the design.

The logo in this design wasn't the client's logo. It is just a placeholder logo that I made.
